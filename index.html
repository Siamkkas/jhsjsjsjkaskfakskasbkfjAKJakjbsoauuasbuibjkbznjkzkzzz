Bridging Two Worlds

Arc is engineered to be the synthesis of the new and the established, combining the efficiency of blockchain with the stability and compliance of regulated finance.

The Challenge

Slow, Opaque Payments

Cross-border transactions take days to clear through a complex web of intermediaries, creating delays and counterparty risk.

Fragmented Markets

Assets are siloed in different jurisdictions with incompatible rails, burdened by manual processes and layers of middlemen.

The Arc Solution

Instant, Global Settlement

Arc provides sub-second, final settlement 24/7, enabling programmable, real-time capital mobility anywhere in the world.

Unified, Tokenized Markets

By supporting tokenized RWAs, Arc creates a single, global venue for asset issuance, trading, and settlement with automated, on-chain logic.

The Challenge

Volatile Gas Fees

Using speculative tokens for gas creates unpredictable operational costs and accounting nightmares for businesses.

Probabilistic Finality

Risk of chain "reorgs" means high-value transactions aren't truly final for minutes or hours, introducing unacceptable settlement risk.

Lack of Compliant Privacy

Fully transparent ledgers expose sensitive commercial data, making them unsuitable for most real-world enterprise use cases.

The Arc Solution

Stable, Predictable Fees

Using USDC as native gas pegs transaction costs to a stable unit of account, enabling predictable financial planning.

Deterministic Finality

Transactions are 100% final and irreversible in under one second, providing the institutional-grade certainty required for finance.

Opt-In, Auditable Privacy

Arc shields transaction values from the public while providing "view keys" for compliant auditing, balancing confidentiality with regulatory needs.

The Three Pillars of Arc

Arc's architecture is a purpose-built foundation for modern finance. Click each pillar to explore the detailed technical innovations within.

1. Stable Economic Model

+

Arc's economy is designed for predictability and stability, removing barriers to enterprise adoption.

  • USDC Native Gas: Aligns the unit of account for value transfer with the unit for transaction cost, eliminating volatility.
  • Predictable Fee Market: Inspired by EIP-1559, Arc uses a fee smoothing mechanism with a bounded base fee to dampen short-term volatility and keep costs consistently low.
  • On-Chain Treasury: Fees are directed to a network treasury to fund long-term growth and ecosystem development, creating a sustainable model.

2. High-Performance Consensus

+

Arc provides the institutional-grade settlement assurances required to bring trillions of dollars of financial activity on-chain.

  • Malachite BFT Engine: A high-performance implementation of Tendermint that provides Byzantine Fault Tolerance, enabling high throughput (3,000-10,000+ TPS).
  • Deterministic Finality: Unlike Proof-of-Work, a transaction on Arc is either unconfirmed or 100% final and irreversible in under a second. There is no probabilistic state.
  • Permissioned Validator Set: The network is secured by a known, vetted set of geographically distributed institutions, providing a strong deterrent against malicious behavior and aligning with emerging regulatory frameworks.

3. Compliant Privacy Framework

+

Arc's phased, modular approach to privacy delivers confidentiality without compromising on institutional compliance.

  • Phased Rollout: Begins with auditable confidential transfers (shielded amounts) and evolves towards fully private state and computation for complex financial logic.
  • Modular & Future-Proof: Leverages Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) initially for performance, with a "pluggable backend" to integrate future technologies like FHE or ZK-proofs as they mature.
  • "View Keys" for Compliance: The core of the model. Institutions retain control and can grant authorized third parties (like auditors) read-only access to specific transaction data, satisfying regulatory obligations like the Travel Rule.
